LODHA AMARA

Lodha Amara Homes are crafted to fulfill all your living needs, from discovering nature to relaxing in its beauty. Located in Kolshet Road West, one of Thane's most esteemed areas, Lodha Homes offer a multitude of outdoor areas designed to offer a diverse range of outdoor activities, including a sports arena, private forest, and open-air cafes. Each floor plan has been thoughtfully designed to maximize space utilization and provide optimal convenience to homeowners. The specifications cater to the needs of modern homebuyers, making Amara an exceptional option for those seeking a meticulously planned project by a trusted developer in an attractive location.
